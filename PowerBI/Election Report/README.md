# 2015 Nigeria Presidential Elections Analysis

## Project Overview

This data analysis project aims to provide insights into the voting pattern across the country. By analyzing various aspects of the elections data, we seek to ascertain the level of voter apathy, make data-driven recommendations, and gain a deeper understanding of states, regional, geopolitical zone, and political parties performance.

To ascertain the level of voter apathy and understand ways to mitigate against it in future elections

### Recommedations
- Fear of violence: In the build-up to elections, politicians should refrain from utterances that could heat up the polity.
- Failed promises: Elected political office holders must ensure to keep to their electioneering compaign promises
- Lack of awareness: The National Orientation Agency must embark on more sensitization programmes before elections
- Disenfranchisement: The electoral umpire must ensure adequate and well-functioning equipment
